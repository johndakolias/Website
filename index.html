JOHN DAKOLIAS

SINGER & SONGWRITER

John Dakolias

John Dakolias is a Singer/Songwriter with more than 5M views on Youtube and more than 5M streams on Spotify. Some of his songs have been featured in channels such as Trap Nation, Trap City and many more, and have been supported by The Chainsmokers, R3hab, Quintino and other major artists. Singing in a Pop style makes his Pop vocals easy to blend in with many different genres and styles of music, thus he’s worked with many different producers for a lot of different types and categories of music. Some of them are Pop, R&B, EDM, Trap, Pop/Rock, Latin Urban, Electropop and Alternative Pop etc..
